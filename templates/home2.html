Consent Form

What is our study about?

The purpose of our study is to gauge the efficacy of automatically generated interfaces in helping Internet browsers understand the content they come across. We will present you with multiple types of graphs as well as at times ask you to use a prototype of our program. The prototype will be hosted online and will not require any installation.

What will you do in this study?

If you agree to participate in our study, you will answer some multiple-choice and short-answer questions about graphs that we will provide you with. You will complete this task guided by a web application. You will interact with and interpret a visualization provided by the experimenters. We log your interactions in your web browser, including clicks, scrolls, and other actions. We are NOT able to view your browser history or other websites you have opened during the study. The information we collect will be used to judge the efficacy of and improve our program.

How long will this study take?

We anticipate the study will take at most 25 minutes.

What risks are associated with this study?

Loss of Confidentiality: Data we collect, including question responses and user actions in our web application, will only be shared amongst members of our research team. Data will be deleted after our study. Research records will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law. Research records may also be reviewed by the Temple Institutional Review Board.

What are alternatives to participating in the study?

You are not under any obligation to participate in the study. If at any time you choose to end participation in the study, you may notify a researcher, and we will delete your data.

What benefits/compensation can you expect?

You will be compensated with $10 for participating in our study.

Ending participation in our study

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may end participation early at any time without penalty or loss of the benefits/compensation to which you are entitled. If you decide to end participation early, you may notify a researcher, and we will delete your data.

Other conditions of withdrawal

The PI may remove you from the study without your consent if the PI feels it is in your best interest or the best interest of the study. If you end your session and cannot reconnect, we will remove you from the study.

Are there any associated costs?

There will be no costs that you will be responsible for.
